About Us

We are Adam and Joanna and this is our Bar and Grill.

We are located at 1818 E 9th Ave, Tampa, FL 33605, near the Ybor museum.


"Our business has been passed down for decades from father to son (or in this case, Adam to Adam). We think it's best that we are able to keep our tradition alive through our family and strong roots in southern cooking and exquisite cocktails."


Adam's French 75

  • 3 oz champagne
  • half oz simple syrup
  • half oz lemon juice

Adam's Sazerac

  • 2 oz rye whiskey
  • 3 dashes Peychaud's Bitters
  • Half oz simple syrup

  • garnish with a lemon twist

Adam's Grasshopper

  • 3/4 oz green creme de menthe
  • 3/4 oz white creme de cacao
  • 3/4 oz light cream
  • garnish with candied cherry
